# **Step 1: Access Meta Business Suite**

Go to [Meta Business Suite](https://business.facebook.com/) and log in using your Facebook credentials.

## <img height="460" width="528" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXd3uXqA4wWqQ8ObVEdyhFCzKr0XYLyLU9nefeLZjXoTUFu45tOa1u2Q_QWs7Ft3ivEfnyfoaHqNhLFSylwM0SnBaYOfe_nchx3EQJ2M38B8bR6tLocxCzn4o-7DvuhUqf5jjxGVhA?key=DsO5aCYLViuTIwgTeDoWM9kW" />**Step 2: Select Your Business Portfolio**

* Click on the dropdown menu at the top left.

* Select the **Business Portfolio** that contains your WhatsApp API account.

* Click on the **Business Settings** icon under **Business Assets**.

  <img height="425" width="495" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdEL80_Db93DI_PnLs-ut_quBTzFe7c1qN5dzp8jrMr5tfTqkD2mZExSDESL9Vo3cODzWEwEMDKm_FY9Ewld4bPraLrW8Wah0elF6gpok0HzX4oLE8qhnhKZVIvM6BFBhxQZW8yDA?key=DsO5aCYLViuTIwgTeDoWM9kW" />

## **Step 3: Navigate to WhatsApp Accounts**

* In **Business Settings**, locate and click on **WhatsApp Accounts**.

<img height="507" width="208" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXd_r9e2JvBsP7vqznwghhC64mokLma4ExX55WaeUjjmt-OFrBn7EuQfD6_SNvPFmKff3XIYM8GZxIEsXe4czVO6Ivgdev1yI4bXQ--gnu9tYoTZHRR0BLumxxbgtov492Glcocd?key=DsO5aCYLViuTIwgTeDoWM9kW" />

## **Step 4: Select Your WhatsApp API Account**

* Click on your **WhatsApp API Account**.

* On the right panel, scroll down to the **WhatsApp Manager** section.

<img height="265" width="602" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXcnf9pNxWWjifgO7qUV273N0QTMG0PjROH2HZMpt8jpBZdMIi1wsLH5UQdihyM8FntLHj3FjmRqHfoTfCjXf1y6nc2jzrPnCFJdNHz2e_RZpiFUTc6Z5G0hrkQIcZ0WGL6e1m25Cg?key=DsO5aCYLViuTIwgTeDoWM9kW" />

## **Step 5: Open WhatsApp Manager**

* Click on **WhatsApp Manager** to open a new tab.

<img height="265" width="602" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXcnf9pNxWWjifgO7qUV273N0QTMG0PjROH2HZMpt8jpBZdMIi1wsLH5UQdihyM8FntLHj3FjmRqHfoTfCjXf1y6nc2jzrPnCFJdNHz2e_RZpiFUTc6Z5G0hrkQIcZ0WGL6e1m25Cg?key=DsO5aCYLViuTIwgTeDoWM9kW" />

## **Step 6: Update Your Business Profile**

* Click on your **Phone Number**.

* Navigate to the **Profile** section.

* Update the necessary business details.

* Click **Save** to apply the changes.
