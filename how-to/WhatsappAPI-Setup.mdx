#### **Step 1: Keep the Prerequisites Ready**

Before starting the onboarding process, make sure you have the following items:

- A **Facebook Account** or **Facebook Business Manager**
- A **Phone Number** (must be new or not currently linked to an active WhatsApp account)
- **Website Link**
- **Email ID**
- Any **one** of the following **Company Certificates**:
  - GST Certificate
  - MSME Certificate
  - Certificate of Incorporation
  - Registration Certificate
  - Bank Statement
  - Utility Bill
