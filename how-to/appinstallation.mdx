## **Help & Support – Installing Wylto App**

### **📌 How to Install Wylto App on Web (Desktop/PC)**

1. Open your browser and go to [**inbox.wylto.com**]().

2. Log in with your credentials.

3. Navigate to the **Settings** tab.

4. Click on **Install App** to complete the installation.

***

### **📌 How to Install Wylto App on Mobile (Android & iOS)**

#### **📱 Android:**

* Open the **Google Play Store**.

* Search for **Wylto** or click [here](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.wylto.app\&pcampaignid=web_share).

* Download and install the app.

#### **🍏 iOS (iPhone) – Temporary Installation** *(Until Available on App Store)*

1. Open **Safari/Chrome** on your iPhone.

2. Enter [**inbox.wylto.com**]() in the address bar.

3. Log in with your credentials.

4. Tap the **Share** button.

5. Select **Add to Home Screen**.

6. The Wylto App will be added to your home screen for easy access.
