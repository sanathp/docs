#### **Step 3:**

At the top-left corner, click on the **Business Portfolio dropdown**.\
Select the business portfolio where your **WhatsApp API account** is added.\
Then, click on the **Business Settings** icon under **Business Assets**.

#### **Step 4:**

After clicking **Business Settings**, a new settings panel will open.

![AD_4nXcvzR0ZV5sF8-KIlEtcDmT6PS7Xaopd_zLFzswI1SRHN0Gk0IXVGgzbwjoWLlM-0oJD5sGNbJ9TAaf3g4gfyrJKQzSDEAyJ23TuNvX-8IDm09Q10BqSpDeH.png](/images/AD_4nXcvzR0ZV5sF8-KIlEtcDmT6PS7Xaopd_zLFzswI1SRHN0Gk0IXVGgzbwjoWLlM-0oJD5sGNbJ9TAaf3g4gfyrJKQzSDEAyJ23TuNvX-8IDm09Q10BqSpDeH.png)

#### **Step 5:**

In the left-hand menu, go to **WhatsApp Accounts**.

#### **Step 6:**

Click on your **WhatsApp API Account**.\
On the right side, scroll down to the **WhatsApp Manager** section.
